The Root Cause of Live Stream Stuttering

Unlike pre-recorded movies (like Netflix) which can pre-load or "buffer" minutes ahead, **live sports broadcasts are delivered in real-time, micro-second data packets**. If your Smart TV or streaming device is connected via Wi-Fi, it faces continuous interference from household walls, mobile phones, and local radio frequencies.

When thousands of data packets drop over wireless airwaves, your screen immediately freezes or drops to 720p resolution. The most effective, industry-standard way to guarantee zero frame drops is to switch from a wireless connection to a **dedicated, shielded physical cable path**.
